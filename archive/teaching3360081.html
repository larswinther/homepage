Grades

The graded work comprises homework assignments, two (2) midterm exams and the final exam.
Grades are posted at WebCT, they count towards the final grade with weights as follows:

Homework:  35%
Midterm exams:  40%  (20% each)
Final exam:  25%

Final grades will be assigned according to the following percentages:

A+  100–97%   A  96.9–93%   A÷  92.9–90%
B+  89.9–87% B  86.9–83% B÷  82.9–80%
C+  79.9–77% C  76.9–73% C÷  72.9–70%
D+  69.9–67% D  66.9–63% D÷  62.9–60%   F  59.9–0%
