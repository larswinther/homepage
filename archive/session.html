Commutative Algebra

AMS Special Session
University of Nebraska-Lincoln, October 21–23, 2005

Format

This session will be comprised of ten 45-minute talks.

The goal of the session is to paint a picture of current research trends in commutative algebra.

According to the tentative schedule, the session starts
at 1:40 PM on Friday and ends at 11:30 AM on Sunday.
