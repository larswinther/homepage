Grades

The graded work comprises homework assignments (10), projects (4), and exams (4). Grades are posted on Blackboard; they count towards the final grade with weights as follows:

Homework:  30%
Projects:  12%
In-class exams:  30%  (10% each)
Final exam:  20%
Attendance:  8%

Final grades will be assigned according to the following percentages:
