Grades

The graded work comprises homework assignments (24) and exams (3). Grades are posted on Blackboard; they count towards the final grade with weights as follows:

Homework:  60%  (2.5% ea)
Participation:  15%  (1% per week)
Term exams:  10%  (5% ea)
Final exam:  15%

Final grades will be assigned according to the following percentages:

A+  100–97%   A  96.9–93%   A÷  92.9–90%    
B+  89.9–87% B  86.9–83% B÷  82.9–80%    
C+  79.9–77% C  76.9–73% C÷  72.9–70%    
D+  69.9–67% D  66.9–63% D÷  62.9–60%   F  59.9–0%
