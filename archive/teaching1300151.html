Grades

The graded work comprises homework assignments (the best 20 out of a total of 25) and exams (2).
Grades are posted at MyMathLab, they count towards the final grade with weights as follows:
Online homework:  40%  (2% each)
Exams:  60%  (30% each)

Final grades will be assigned according to the following percentages:
