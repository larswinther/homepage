Grades

The graded work comprises homework assignments (10), projects (4), and three (3) exams. Grades are posted on Blackboard; they count towards the final grade with weights as follows:

Homework:  30%
Projects:  12%
In-class exams:  30%  (15% each)
Final exam:  20%
Attendance:  8%

Final grades will be assigned according to the following percentages:

A+  100–97%   A  96.9–93%   A÷  92.9–90%
B+  89.9–87% B  86.9–83% B÷  82.9–80%
C+  79.9–77% C  76.9–73% C÷  72.9–70%
D+  69.9–67% D  66.9–63% D÷  62.9–60%   F  59.9–0%
