Registration

To ease the planning we request that all participants fill out the registration form before 11 February 2013.

Travel and Accommodation

The conference takes place in the Richard A. Harvill Building (1103 E. 2nd St., Tucson, AZ 85721). Free weekend parking is available in the Park Avenue Garage, (1140 N. Park Ave., Tucson, AZ 85721). These are buildings 76 and 116 on the campus map .
The party will be at Gentle Ben's Brewing Co. Directions on how to get there will be in your participant's package.

Rooms have been blocked at the River Park Inn. To book a room at the conference rate of $89/night plus tax, please fill in the hotel section of the registration form.

Sponsors and Support

The meeting is supported by funds from the National Science Foundation and from the Department of Mathematics at the University of Arizona.
