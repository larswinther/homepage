Registration

To ease planning and reporting we request that all participants fill out the registration form before 11 February 2016. Early registrants have priority for support , and you must register by 15 January 2016 to be eligible for support.

Travel and Accommodation

The talks will take place in Derrick Hall, 105 Pickard Street; it is next to the Math department, the building marked DERR on the campus map . On Weekends parking is free except for reserved spots. A convenient place to park is the Matthews Street Garage.

The conference hotel is Holiday Inn San Marcos located at 105 Bintu Drive, San Marcos, TX 78666. Please register by 15 January 2016 to book a room at the conference rate.

The social gathering on Saturday takes place at the home of Professor Max Warshauer. Directions will be provided in your participant's package.

From the Austin airport (AUS) there is shuttle service to San Marcos and several rental car options. If you drive, download directions .
