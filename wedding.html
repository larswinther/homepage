Wedding of Carmen and Lars

Pazo de Cea, Nigrán, 28 July 2016

Venue

The wedding takes place on the Atlantic coast of Northwest Spain, in Nigrán, which sits south of the port city of Vigo and north of the historic fishermen's town of Baiona.
Pazo de Cea is a XVI century manor in the country side of Galicia; it has beautiful gardens and the Portuguese branch of the Camino de Santiago crosses its grounds.

Dress code

The dress code is "Formal"; that means dresses and suits for certain, but long gowns and tuxedos are optional.

The weather in Galicia can be unpredictable, but no matter what it cools down after sunset in Nigrán because of the proximity to the ocean. The temperature may well be 25–30 C at the time of the ceremony but drop below 15 C during the night.

How to get there

The closest airport is Vigo (VGO). The airport in Porto (OPO) is better connected, and there is bus service (1.5 hour) from OPO to Vigo. There is also an airport in Santiago de Compostela (SCQ), and one can get from Santiago to Vigo by train (1 hour). Vigo also has train connections to Madrid and Lisbon.

Where to stay

Vigo has a large selection of hotels, but for those who want to enjoy the beautiful beaches we recommend staying in Baiona or Nigrán. The latter has a gorgeous beach, but a limited selection of hotels and restaurants. Baiona has a lovely historical downtown, and a wider selection of restaurants and hotels.

After the party, there will be bus transportation to Baiona with stops at Hotel Bahia Bayona, at Hotel Pazo de Mendoza, and at the Parador de Baiona. See also "Transportation to Pazo de Cea".

Hotels in the area fill up fast. We have blocked rooms in two of the hotels mentioned above, and they can be accessed as follows:
Pazo de Mendoza: write to pazodemendoza@pazodemendoza.com before 1 June and use code "boda Carmen-Lars"
Parador de Baiona: write to commercial.baiona@parador.es before 28 April and use code "Boda Carmen y Lars". They are all double rooms with breakfast and ocean view.

Boutique hotels in Baiona include Hotel Anunciada, Hotel Carabela La Pinta, and Hotel Tres Carabelas.

Children

Children of all ages are welcome. For those who need it, professional child care is available at the Pazo de Cea. They can take care of babies and entertain older children.

Transportation to Pazo de Cea

The Pazo has ample parking space for those who arrive by car, and there will be staff on hand to arrange for taxis during the night.

For the people staying in or around Baiona, there will be a bus leaving for Pazo de Cea from the Parador de Baiona at 18.00 (6 pm), stopping on the way at the Pazo de Mendoza, and at Hotel Bahia de Baiona from where it departs at 18.15 (6.15 pm). During the night, the bus will make two return trips from the Pazo de Cea to Baiona; they are tentatively scheduled for 2 am and 4 am.
